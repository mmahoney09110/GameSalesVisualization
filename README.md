# Game Sales Visualization

**Author:** Robert Mahoney  
**Date:** April 24, 2025  

---

## Project Overview
This Tableau visualization project explores global video game sales to understand which factors drive success. Key focuses include platform trends, developer reach, genre popularity, and regional preferences. The analysis is intended for video game market analysts and publishing executives.  

---

## Data
- Source: [Maven Analytics Video Game Dataset](https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground?order=number_of_records%2Cdesc&pageSize=20&search=Video%20Game)  
- Covers global sales, developers, publishers, platforms, genres, critic scores, and more  
- Challenges addressed:  
  - Null values in some fields  
  - Segmenting games by region  
  - Inconsistent release dates  

---

## Key Findings
1. **Platform & Developer Trends**  
   - Broader developer reach and popular consoles like PS2 drive higher cumulative sales  
   - Top developers publishing across multiple consoles dominate best-seller lists  

2. **Genre & Quality Influence**  
   - Sports, Action, and Shooter consistently top sales in all regions  
   - Higher critic scores (>9) correlate with massive sales  
   - Genre variety has grown alongside sales, appealing to broader audiences  

3. **Global & Market Dynamics**  
   - Market share dominated by top publishers (Activision, EA)  
   - Regional preferences matter: Japan favors RPGs; other regions lean toward Sports, Action, and Shooter genres  

---

## Tableau Dashboards
The workbook contains three story-driven dashboards:  

1. **Console & Developer Insights**  
   - Top 10 Developers, Consoles Worked On, Top Selling Games  
   - Critic Score vs. Sales  

2. **Sales Trends & Console Performance**  
   - Total Games Sold by Console, Game Sales by Year, Variety Through the Years  

3. **Market Influence & Global Preferences**  
   - Top 10 Publishers by Market Share, Most Popular Games Across Regions, Game Sales by Genre
